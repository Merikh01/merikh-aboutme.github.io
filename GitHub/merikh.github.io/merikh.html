About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Merikh. I'm from Manado City, Indonesia. I have an autoimmune condition called psoriasis vulgaris.

I just wanna say — to anyone struggling with things like psoriasis: you are amazing, you are beautiful, and please, keep loving yourself.

All the things that happen to me, I pour into my songs. Music is my way of venting, of healing, of telling the world that we must believe and accept everything that life gives us.

I make music to connect, to speak honestly, and to remind you that you're not alone.
